The United States Air Force (USAF) is the aerial and space warfare branch of the U.S. Armed Forces, established as an independent service in 1947. It plays a crucial role in national defense, with capabilities ranging from air superiority and precision strikes to intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance. The USAF also supports global humanitarian missions, space exploration, and nuclear deterrence, making it one of the most advanced and versatile air forces in the world.
